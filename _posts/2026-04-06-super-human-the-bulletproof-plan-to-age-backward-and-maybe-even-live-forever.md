### Synopsis

*"No supplement can replicate the full spectrum of light humans need from the sun."*

*Super Human* (2019) is a guide to the latest research in longevity. By making basic changes in your diet and adopting cutting-edge anti-aging technology, you can bypass aging for decades to come while looking as good as you feel.

## What’s in it for me? Hack your biology to radically extend your lifespan.

Picture two versions of yourself at a dinner party twenty years from now. The first version is complaining about aching joints, failing memory, and endless medications, aging like previous generations. The second version is feeling better than ever, sharper, more energetic, and biologically younger. Science fiction? Not anymore.

The guide is Dave Asprey. His own health struggles led him to discover that most aging issues are not genetic fate but mitochondrial dysfunction, which is reversible. Learn about effective anti-aging techniques.

## Blink 1 - Biological aging is caused by mitochondrial degeneration.

Mitochondria are like microscopic power plants in your cells, producing energy. As you age, these power plants weaken, creating cellular damage and chronic inflammation, leading to diseases associated with aging. Keeping free radicals and antioxidants balanced is key to slowing aging.

By supporting your mitochondria, you can reverse aging and improve your health significantly.

## Blink 2 - Avoid dying by adopting a low-carb diet with the right proteins and fats.

The western diet can harm mitochondria, leading to chronic inflammation and accelerated aging. Shift to a high-fat, low-carb diet with the right proteins to support mitochondrial health. Grass-fed meat, olive oil, avocado, and omega-3 fats are essential for a healthy diet.

What you eat greatly impacts your mitochondria and overall health.

## Blink 3 - A cyclical ketogenic diet and intermittent fasting are cheap strategies for healthy brain function.

Fuel your body with a high-fat, low-carb diet to boost brain function and overall health. Consider adopting a cyclical ketogenic diet and intermittent fasting to enhance your metabolism and overall well-being.

Timing your meals and consuming the right foods can significantly impact your health.

## Blink 4 - Learn which kind of light to avoid and when.

Avoiding sunlight entirely is as harmful as excessive exposure. Get enough sunlight daily for vitamin D production but protect your skin from overexposure. Limit blue light exposure from screens to improve sleep quality and overall health.

Balancing sunlight exposure and managing blue light exposure can benefit your health.

## Blink 5 - Use sleep aids to get the right amount of quality sleep.

Quality sleep is crucial for overall health and rejuvenation. Monitor and optimize your sleep cycles for maximum restorative benefits. Track your sleep patterns and consider aids like the Sleep Cycle app for better sleep quality.

Good sleep is essential for cellular repair and general well-being.

## Blink 6 - To age backwards, regulate your hormones with hormone replacement therapy or high-intensity interval training.

Maintain optimal hormone levels for better health and longevity. Consider hormone replacement therapy under specialist guidance or try high-intensity interval training to naturally boost hormone levels.

Balancing your hormones is vital for overall health and aging gracefully.

## Blink 7 - Stem cells treatment offers Super Human healing and disease prevention.

Stem cell therapy shows promise for healing and rejuvenating the body. Replenishing and reactivating stem cells can promote tissue repair and overall well-being. While stem cell treatments are currently expensive, storing harvested stem cells for future use may become more accessible in the future.

Harnessing the power of stem cells can revolutionize healing and disease prevention.

## Blink 8 - Look younger by boosting your collagen levels and maintaining your natural hair.

Combat wrinkles and aging by boosting collagen levels through diet and supplements. Address hair issues by supporting melanin production and addressing potential hormonal imbalances that could contribute to hair loss. Optimize your diet and lifestyle for youthful skin and hair.

Healthy habits and dietary choices can improve your appearance and overall health.

## Final summary

Understanding mitochondrial efficiency is key to longevity. Adopting a healthy lifestyle including diet, sleep, hormone management, and stem cell therapy can reverse aging and improve overall health and appearance. Aging doesn't have to be inevitable when you make informed choices for your well-being.
